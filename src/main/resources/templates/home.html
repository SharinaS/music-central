Adding a Musician Is Easy...

  • Easily add a name and/or a link to an image you'd like to use.
  • Add the info you'd like to add; you're not required to fill in all the fields.
  • If you don't have your own URL, you may do a Google image search.
  • Click on the image you'd like to use, right-click over the image, and click "copy image address."
  • Paste that image address as the image you'd like to be associated with the musician.
  • Do note that images that have vertical orientation look better than those with horizontal orientation.

Adding Songs - Hints

  • It's easy to add in the title of the song - you can name it whatever you like!
  • To add a link to the YouTube song, go to YouTube.
  • Search for the song, click on the song so it's playing on YouTube, and then copy the YouTube link on the top of the page.
  • Go back to MusicCentral, and paste in the YouTube link.
  • To add an image to your song, you can use a URL to an image you own, or you can go to Google.
  • Do a search for the song, and click on "Images" on the top of the Google search page.
  • Click on the image you want to use.
  • Hover over the large image that displays after clicking on it, and right-click so a drop down of options appears. Scroll down to "Copy Image Address."
  • Go back to MusicCentral and paste in the image URL into the form provided.
  • Do note that images that have vertical orientation look better than those with horizontal orientation
